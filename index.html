Qiong Wu

Qiong Wu is a Senior Inventive Scientist at AT&T Labs Research. Her research focuses on developing robust machine learning algorithms under harsh conditions, such as an excessively large number of features and notoriously low signal-to-noise ratio.

Before joining AT&T, Qiong obtained her Ph.D. in computer science at William & Mary. She received her B.Eng degree from the Dalian University of Technology and M.Sc degree from the University of Hong Kong.
